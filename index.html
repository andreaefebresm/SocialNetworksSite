At the beginning of 2019, according to the report Digital 2019, the digital users spent more than 2 hours and 16 minutes per day on Social Networks. Moreover Global Digital Yearbook, carried out by Hootsuite and We Are Social, points out the 44 most social countries in the World. These countries exceed the 36 minutes average amount of time per day spent on Social Networks via any device. Referring to the Human Geography (2008) of Jerome D. Fellmann, the World can be divided in 11 macro-regions that share cultural and historical experience. A further division was made for Europe, which was split in three parts: Northern, Southern and Middle European cultural area.

1 — Which types of Social Network are used in the world?

The dataset considers 43 Social Networks that has been clustered in five families by type of contents: Blogging, Video Hosting, Messaging, Image Sharing and Job Networking. On the Y axis, each family has its Social Networks ordered by ranking, from the most to the least used in the World. Every Social has its own simplified icon and its representative colour. Horizontally, there are the cultural areas listed by geographical order; each one of them has its countries ordered by Social Network Penetration.
